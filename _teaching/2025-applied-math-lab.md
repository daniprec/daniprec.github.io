A practical programming course for students in the Bachelor of Applied Mathematics at IE University. The course uses Python to work through topics at the intersection of mathematics and data science: matrix operations, least squares, data analysis, visualization, and an introduction to machine learning.

Course materials are publicly available. Browse lectures, exercises, and projects on the [course website](https://danielprecioso.com/BAM-Applied-Math-Lab/).
